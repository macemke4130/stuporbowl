Trans Gender-Non Conforming & Women's Group Ride

When

Thursday, February 6th, 2025 5pm

Where

Behind Bars Bicycle Shop
208 13th Ave NE, Minneapolis, MN

Event Details

Starting the Stupor weekend off right with a trans/gnc/womens social ride!

You do NOT have to be racing the event to participate! This ride is for any cyclists that fall under the trans, gender non-conforming, and woman identities.

Ride will meet at Behind Bars bike shop in northeast Minneapolis. Come hang and meet new friends before the drunkest race in the country!
